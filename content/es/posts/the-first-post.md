Hello World!

I took the liberty of writing the first post for the Project Tzib website. This project was born from the idea of equiping the archeology school from the University of San Carlos of Guatemala (USAC) with tools that would be too expensive to acquire via purchases or subscriptions. However, this project is **not** limited to help USAC archeology school, but is destined to disponibilize technology to any academic unit that is in need of the tools we will work in our catalog.

In Guatemala exists lots of archological sites that doesn't have the same pull as *Tikal* or *La Danta*, where most of the tourism and studies happen. Contributing to Tzib Projects helps the archeologists in charge to this lesser-known but important archeological sites to get quality information for their studies in search of discovering more data about the civilizations that inhabited our planet before us.

If you feel intrigued to the work that is starting here, and you want to either colaborate or make use of our projects feel free to contact us, we will be happy to hear from you! 

Hope to hear from potential users/contributors in the future, much regards

-Pablo García.
